What is Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Intelligence, aka EQ, is the ability to read one's emotions/feelings and to empathise them.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ, which stands for Intelligence Quotaient, is measure of problem-solving and reasoning skills. IQ can be a measure to see how 'intelligent' or 'logical' one is while EQ is a measure to see how one can notice and empathise their own or others' emotions.

Why is it important?

EQ is a cruical trait to have for humans and in their society/community they're in. Being able to understand emotions, one can build stronger relationships with others as well as oneself. Having higher EQ helps one to be a better communicator.
