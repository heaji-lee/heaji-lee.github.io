Understanding neuroplasticity and its benefits to people

Neuroplasticity refers to "the brain's ability to modify, change, and adapt both structure and function throughout life and in response to experience". Some studies have shown that the brains aren't in a fixed form, but rather are malleable throughout life. Understanding this, people understand that there are no limits in absorbing or studying new information and are more resilient.

Engage with neuroplaticity for my benefit

I used to think that my brain has a set capacity and that I could not "take in" more information than the capacity. With the fact my brain has unlimited space to store information, I can push my limits.

Ways to increase your neuroplasticity

Practice makes permanent. Repeat an activity over and over help you to build stronger relationship with the activity and you become more comfortable doing them. Keep reminding yourself you are capable of unlimited things will make you more motivated. Embrace the fact that you don't know an information or how to do a certain thing and focus on improving them.

What is Growth Mindset and why is it relevant?

Growth mindset is knowing that you can improve your skills with practice and enough effort. The saying "know your limits" does not apply to growth mindset as limits don't exist in improving your skills. Understanding that abilities can be developed is important so you don't get trapped in a box saying "Oh, you can't do this" or "Oh, you'll never be as good as this or that person"
